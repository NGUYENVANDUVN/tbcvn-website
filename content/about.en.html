TBC: Thien Binh Colour

TBC stands for "Thien Binh Colour", inspired by the colour of the Libra constellation, symbolising creation and sustainable development. We bring the energy of peace, so you can freely create a bright, potential future.

Creating Timeless Furniture

At TBC Furniture, we merge traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation to craft exceptional furniture components. Our mission is to provide the furniture industry with high-quality, durable products while fostering creativity and sustainability in every piece we produce.
